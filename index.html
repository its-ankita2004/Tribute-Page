Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam

Missile Man of India • Visionary • Beloved President

Dr. Avul Pakir Jainulabdeen Abdul Kalam was an Indian aerospace scientist and the 11th President of India from 2002 to 2007. He was widely referred to as the "Missile Man of India" for his work on the development of ballistic missile and launch vehicle technology.

What I truly admire about Dr. Kalam is his humility and deep passion for inspiring the youth. Despite reaching the highest office in the nation, he always remained grounded, dedicating his life to science, education, and national development.

His words, like “Dream, dream, dream. Dreams transform into thoughts and thoughts result in action,” have left a lasting impact on millions. He emphasized the power of education and hard work, teaching us to strive for excellence regardless of background.

Dr. Kalam passed away doing what he loved — teaching — and he remains an enduring symbol of hope, perseverance, and service to the nation.

“You have to dream before your dreams can come true.” – Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam
